The E.ON Homebuyers Guide is a responsive microsite offering new home owners advice on possible energy efficiency savings and money-saving deals their new property

Built, tested, and seeded with content in four weeks, the project was deployed to production via iframe by the client. The Heroku demo site displays the site as intended.
