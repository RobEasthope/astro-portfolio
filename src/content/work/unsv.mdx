Verified is a United Nations initiative, in collaboration with Purpose, to provide life-saving information, fact-based advice and stories as the world copes with the coronavirus pandemic.

Built in a record two and half days flat in time for the Verified global launch by the Secretary-General of the United Nations.

This SSG Next.js app features:

- Full multi-lingual support in nine separate langauges
- Arabic RTL support
- A bespoke Sanity headless CMS studio serving all text content and image assets
- MUX headless video support integrated via the Sanity studio
- ActionKit newsletter sign up and unsubscribe functionality
