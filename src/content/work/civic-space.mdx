Civic Space is a project that aims to provide satellite imagery and data visualization tools for civil society organizations. The project is designed to help organizations monitor and analyze human rights violations, environmental degradation, and other issues that affect communities around the world.

The project is built using React, Remix, and Mapbox. It is hosted on independently controlled servers to ensure data privacy and security. The project is designed to be accessible and user-friendly, and to provide a valuable resource for organizations working to promote social justice and human rights.
