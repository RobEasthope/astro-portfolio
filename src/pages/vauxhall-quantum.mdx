The Vauxhall Quantum Forms project was a complex set of data capture forms to drive Vauxhall’s sales process.

Built on top of Drupal 8 and Drupal Form Builder it needed to connect to multiple API’sand receive data passed in via an iFrame URL, while remaining fully modular.
